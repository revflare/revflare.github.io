AI-Powered SPIN Selling Methodology

Our system uses the proven SPIN framework to qualify leads and create urgency through structured SMS conversations

S

Situation Questions

The AI gathers essential background information to understand the lead's context.

Example:
"What type of HVAC system do you currently have in your home?"
P

Problem Questions

Uncovers issues and pain points the lead is experiencing with their current situation.

Example:
"Are you experiencing uneven temperatures in different rooms?"
I

Implication Questions

Explores the consequences and impact of not addressing the identified problems.

Example:
"How much do you estimate these issues are adding to your energy bills?"
N

Need-Payoff Questions

Helps the lead articulate the value and benefits of solving their problem.

Example:
"How would consistent temperatures throughout your home improve your comfort?"
