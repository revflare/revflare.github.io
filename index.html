Powered by Proven Sales Methodologies

revFlare uses AI that seamlessly switches between SPIN and Sandler techniques to qualify leads based on their responses.

Situation

Gather background information about the lead's current situation

"What prompted you to look into roofing services today?"

Problem

Identify specific issues the lead is experiencing

"Have you noticed any leaks or damage to your current roof?"

Implication

Explore the consequences of not solving their problem

"How has this issue affected your home or business operations?"

Need-Payoff

Establish the value of solving their problem

"How valuable would it be to have this fixed before the rainy season?"

Rapport

Build trust and establish a relationship

"I see you're in the Oakland area. We've completed several projects there recently."

Pain

Uncover the true problems that need solving

"What issues have you been experiencing with your current system?"

Budget

Discuss financial expectations and constraints

"Have you set aside a budget for this project?"

Decision

Identify the decision-making process

"Who else would be involved in making the final decision?"

Fulfillment

Determine expectations for solution delivery

"What timeline are you working with for this project?"

Close

Secure commitment and next steps

"Would you like to schedule an in-person assessment this week?"
